Welcome to AI Prompt Genius

Organize Your AI Prompts

Create, edit, and organize your AI prompts in one place. Use tags to categorize your prompts and find them quickly.

Use Variables in Your Prompts

Create dynamic prompts with variables using the {{variable_name}} syntax. When you use a prompt with variables, you'll be prompted to fill in the values.

Access Your Prompts Anywhere

Use the extension popup, sidebar, or search page to access your prompts from anywhere in your browser.

Keyboard Shortcuts

Open Sidebar: Ctrl+Shift+U (Windows/Linux) or Command+Shift+U (Mac)
Open Search: Ctrl+Shift+P (Windows/Linux) or Command+Shift+P (Mac)
